TREATMENT OF expression wrinkles

Botulinum toxin relaxes certain muscles responsible for wrinkles on the forehead, between the eyebrows, around the eyes and in the neck. It is possible to prevent the effects of aging without altering the natural expressions of the face.

At Praktijk 85, you will find the latest safe and painless injection techniques.

The indications for botulinum toxin allow:

  • Forehead wrinkle treatment
  • Treatment of frown lines
  • Treatment of eye wrinkles
  • Treatment of the chin (mentalis)
  • Treatment of marionettes lines (drooping corners of the lips)
  • Treatment of bruxism (significant contraction of the chewing muscles)
  • Gummy smile treatment
  • neck treatment
  • Treatment of armpit hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating)
  • Expression wrinkle prevention treatment
  • To do a brow lift (eyebrow lift)
General information on Botulinum Toxin

Botulinum toxin comes from the culture of a bacterium called “Clostridium Botulinum”. It blocks the nerve conduction that allows muscle contractions and thus inhibits muscle activity for a specified period. Used as a treatment for certain ophthalmological (strabismus) or neurological (muscle spasms) disorders by thousands of children and adult patients, these aesthetic effects have mainly been noticed during the treatment of blepharospasm (spasms of the eyelid). for more than 20 years, its indications have thus been extended to aesthetic medicine. It is now recognized as a safe and effective treatment for facial expression lines and excess sweating. The dose used in aesthetic medicine is very low compared to therapeutic use.

Removal of expression lines by injection

"Dynamic" or expression wrinkles, such as wrinkles on the forehead, frown (glabella) and crow's feet, result from the repeated contraction of certain muscles. Nerves make and transport a chemical, acetylcholine, which allows muscles to contract. Botulinum toxin blocks the spread of acetylcholine and therefore decreases the contraction of the injected muscles. It differs from all other wrinkle treatments because it does not act on the wrinkles themselves but on the muscles responsible for their appearance. Thanks to botulinum toxin, the skin becomes smoother and the eyes open up in a precise, temporary and reversible way.

Indications for treatment with Botulinum Toxin

In specific areas of the face, expression wrinkles are born by the repetition of certain expressions. In aesthetic medicine, botulinum toxin is the treatment commonly used to reduce these wrinkles. In young people, the skin regains its smooth appearance after each muscle contraction. With age, the skin loses its elasticity but also its firmness, wrinkles gradually deepen and become more visible permanently. This results in a “break” of the dermis which persists.

There are 3 major areas that can be treated:

  • crow’s feet: these wrinkles form at the corner of the eyes and are generated by expressions of smile or laughter.
  • frown lines: they are between the eyebrows. The lion's wrinkle appears when you frown to express dissatisfaction or even a reflection.
  • horizontal wrinkles of the forehead which are wrinkles generated by expressions of widening of the eyes or by surprise.

More incidentally, other areas can be treated but the technique, more difficult, must be perfectly mastered:

  • Fine vertical wrinkles on the lips: they appear when speaking and advancing the lips
  • the folds of marionettes lines on each side of the mouth towards the chin
  • the wrinkles of the nose…
What is a botox injection session like?

Before any treatment, Dr. Marie-Isabelle Al-Awa will perform a careful examination of your face in order to establish a diagnosis. She will then evaluate the facial expressions that are specific to you, the irregularities as well as the symmetry of your face. She will examine the real and suitable need for an injection of botulinum toxin in a way that does not disturb your expressions.

During the treatment, the skin of the face is carefully disinfected, the doctor will draw the planned injection points on the specific muscles responsible for the wrinkles. A tiny amount is injected under the skin a few millimeters deep. Once the injections are finished, you will have to be very careful not to perform any massage to avoid the diffusion of the muscle relaxing injection and not to cause complications.

Results and maintenance

After 10-15 days, the effects of the toxin are visible: spontaneous smoothing of the upper half of the face. The forehead smooths out, loses its severe and sad character. The folds of the crow's feet and the glabella are attenuated and the eyebrows are raised. The look opens and looks less tired.

The first times, the effect of the injections lasts between 4 to 6 months (depending on your initial state). By renewing the sessions without waiting too long (around 2 to 4 times in the first year), the results can last longer. It is not a matter of "paralysis" or muscle destruction that sets in, but we often and gradually lose the habit of frowning these areas.

It is therefore necessary to practice the sessions about 4 to 6 months apart on average. If you want to stop your botulinum toxin injection treatment, your muscles will return to their place, become less and less relaxed and your wrinkles will appear gradually, nothing more.

Contraindications
  • Pregnant women and breast-feeding
  • Neuromuscular pathology: Myasthenia gravis or Lambert-Eaton syndrome.
  • Treatment based on anticoagulant, anti-inflammatory or even antibiotic-based (aminoside)

Skin radiance is often a sign of good health, but it can sometimes be altered by genetic and environmental factors. That is why I offer you different medical techniques to regain a healthy and radiant skin.

Skinboosters

Skin boosters work to improve the skin’s overall quality by boosting hydration levels deep inside the skin and increasing its smoothness, elasticity and firmness. They are specifically formulated to provide deep skin hydration and collagen stimulation of the face, neck, décolletage and neck.

They are suitable for all skin types. Visable improvements to the skin can be seen after an initial course of treatments resulting in healthy skin with a beautiful glow. Whilst providing deep skin hydration this treatment can also help improve the appearance of fine lines and imperfections such as acne scars. This treatment can be used inedependently or as part of an overall treatment programme with other injectable treatments, for example fillers, to help you achieve a natural, fresh look.

For optimal results 2-3 sessions are needed a couple of weeks apart.

Mesotherapy / MicroNeedling

Mesotherapy or "Mesolift" is done with a mixture of non-crosslinked hyaluronic acid and vitamins injected under the skin. The mixture acts in depth, nourishing, hydrating, revitalizing the skin by stimulating microvascularization and collagen production. It is a painless treatment that is widely used in aesthetic medicine.

MicroNeedling consists of piercing the skin at variable depths with tiny needles. The objective of MicroNeedling is to boost collagen, to eliminate old collagen and to start the creation of new organized collagen, the skin thickens and imperfections such as scars and wrinkles are reduced.

Medical peelings associated with an LED session

Superficial to Medium, AHA or TCA, they reach different depths depending on the need. They can act on complexion, spots, fine lines, wrinkles, dilated pores, acne and shallow acne scars. In several sessions, I will try to improve the quality of your skin by complementing it with the appropriate cosmetic care.

Led sessions are used as a complement to stimulate cell energy, promoting collagen synthesis and illuminating the complexion.

LED phototherapy

LED phototherapy is used for facial care, for complexion radiance and for pore tightening. It stimulates the production of collagen and elastin, which are essential for the skin's elasticity, and improves blood circulation and detoxification. It also improves the quality of the skin by a progressive restructuring following the LED session.

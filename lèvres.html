Lip volumization

The smile is the key element of your face, but over time the lip contour can become marked by wrinkles or fine lines. Hyaluronic acid lip treatment can be used to reshape the lip contour or to increase the volume of the lips.

Hyaluronic acid injections can be used to treat thinning of the lip border, to compensate for a loss of volume in the mouth or to fight against dry lips.

Treatment indications

  • Lip contour treatment
  • Treatment of loss of lip volume
  • Definition of the cupid's bow (philtrum)
  • Treatment of the corners of the mouth
  • Treatment of fine lines on the lips
  • Treatment of dry lips
  • Treatment of the gummy smile
  • Correction of asymmetries

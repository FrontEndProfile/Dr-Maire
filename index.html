about me

about me

Doctor Marie-Isabelle Al-Awa graduated Medicine in 2015 with honours. In the aim to better her patients' well being, she pursued further studies in aesthetic medicine in Paris.

After receiving her certification she was recognised by the Belgian Society of Aesthetic Doctors (BVEG) in 2016 and opened later in 2017 her own clinic in Waterloo. What drove Dr. Al-Awa's dedication to aesthetic medicine was the intersection of her knowledge in health, her passion for beauty, and the finesse of the technical interventions.

Dr. Al-Awa not only values technical results but also a holistic approach in treating her patients. As aesthetic medicine has taken an increasingly important place in a patient's pursuit of health and well-being, it is crucial to choose the given interventions with care. Enhancing your own natural beauty is the philosophy of the house. This starts with a thorough first consultation mandatory for all (no exceptions). Where a physical examination along with discussing the patient's needs, desired results, and expectations is done. The doctors might proceed with offering a treatment plan according to what is best suited for her patients. (*)

We deeply value beauty at all ages. This is why we work by remedying but we also work in prevention to avoid late-on unwanted effects such as aging signs etc. As mentioned previously, in this clinic it is all about "enhancement".The aim is not to change your entire identity but to respect, flourish into your identity. It is not about the quantity of products but the quality of products and care.

Patients come with very diverse cases, some are concerned about specific facial expressions, such as looking too sad, too tired, too angry, older or less feminine, less masculine than what they feel from the inside. These are all important concerns to discuss, and they are welcomed by all genders as well. "Mens sana in corpore sano". A healthy mind in a healthy body is deeply valued in Aesthetic Medicine. Dr. Al-awa believes that one affects the other. A step towards a healthy mind is a step towards a healthy body, and vice versa. Aesthetic medicine offers an alternative to some plastic surgery interventions or be an option to a post operative correction. While giving the option to grow almost naturally into your inner and outer beauty.
